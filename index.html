Spiderman.

"With Great Power Comes Great Responsibility"

Spider-Man is a superhero. With spider-like abilities, science genius swings above it all as Spider-Man, costumed champion of the innocent who lives and fights with the wisdom of “With Great Power Comes Great Responsibility!”

Who is Spiderman ?

Alright, let's do this...My name is Peter Parker. I was bitten by a radioactive spider, And for ten years, I've been the one and only Spider-Man. I’m pretty sure you know the rest. Look, I'm a comic book, I'm a cereal, did a Christmas album. I have an excellent theme song. I mean, I've looked worse. But after everything, I still love being Spider-Man. I mean, who wouldn't? So no matter how many hits I take, I always find a way to come back. Because the only thing standing between this city and oblivion is me. There's only one Spider-Man, and you're looking at him.
